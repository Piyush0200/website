Warm-Up

Start your session with a dynamic warm-up to activate your muscles, increase blood flow, and enhance flexibility. Spend 5-10 minutes on light cardio or dynamic stretches to get your body ready.

Include arm swings, leg kicks, and torso twists. A proper warm-up reduces injury risk and improves performance during your workout.

Strength Training

Focus on major muscle groups with compound movements like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses. Strength training builds muscle, enhances metabolism, and improves overall fitness.

Incorporate a mix of free weights, machines, and bodyweight exercises. Remember to maintain good form to prevent injuries and maximize effectiveness.

Cool Down

End your workout with a 5-10 minute cool-down. Gentle stretching helps relax your muscles, reduces soreness, and improves flexibility.

Focus on slow, deep breathing and hold stretches for at least 20 seconds. Cooling down aids recovery and prepares you for your next session.
