1

Warm-up

Start your workout with a 5-minute warm-up. Engage in light cardio activities such as jogging, cycling, or jumping jacks to increase your heart rate and prepare your muscles for the workout ahead.

2

Strength Training

Proceed with strength training exercises. Perform 3 sets of squats, 3 sets of bench presses, and 3 sets of deadlifts. Ensure you maintain proper form and gradually increase weights to build muscle effectively.

3

Cardio

Incorporate a 20-minute cardio session. Choose activities like running, cycling, or rowing to boost cardiovascular health and endurance. Maintain a moderate to high intensity throughout the session.

4

Cooldown

Finish your workout with a 10-minute cooldown. Engage in stretching exercises targeting all major muscle groups to improve flexibility and reduce muscle soreness. This helps in recovery and prevents injuries.
